How is this score calculated?

This fit score is generated by an intelligent analysis engine designed to simulate a hiring manager's initial review.

  • REQUIREMENT PRIORITIZATION: The system distinguishes between your mandatory requirements and optional "nice-to-haves," weighting the score accordingly.
  • EXPERIENCE VERIFICATION: It verifies that the candidate hasn't just listed a skill, but has actually applied it in the commercial projects showcased in this portfolio.
  • TERMINOLOGY RECOGNITION: It bridges the gap between different job titles and skill names (e.g., recognizing that "Client-side" experience relates to "Frontend").
  • HOLISTIC ASSESSMENT: The final score reflects the overall alignment of the candidate's professional background with the specific needs of your open position.
