How is this score calculated?

This isn't just a keyword counter. The system analyzes the context of your Job Description.

  • CORE SKILLS: We check if the candidate has the "Must-Haves" you listed.
  • SMART MATCHING: It understands that "React" and "ReactJS" are the same thing.
  • PROJECT PROOF: The score is higher if the skill was used in shipped projects.
  • BONUS POINTS: "Nice-to-haves" and extra experience boost the score.
